# web-development
Mamma Ruby’s Bakery is a warm, welcoming, and passionate baking brand that delivers fresh and delicious treats every day. Our website showcases our bakery products, brand identity, and overall customer experience. Everything we bake is made with love, care, and the finest ingredients.

Design overview

The Mamma Ruby’s Bakery website features:

A soft, warm colour palette

A cheerful pink navigation bar

Elegant and readable typography

A neat product gallery displaying cakes, cookies, and cupcakes

A friendly, handcrafted bakery feel

A clean, inviting layout that highlights the brand’s name 


Brand colours and meaning

Pink – nostalgia, warmth, sweetness, joy

Brown – comfort, homeliness, and natural goodness

Wheat – simplicity, wholesomeness, and natural ingredients

These colours work together to create a soft, welcoming brand identity.

our products

We proudly offer:

Cakes

Cookies

Cupcakes

All products are freshly baked and made with high-quality ingredients.

project goals
Our website aims to:

Build human connection and create meaningful customer experiences

Ensure everyone feels valued through an inclusive brand environment

Grow customer loyalty and trust

Make customers’ lives easier, especially during special events such as
weddings, birthdays, and traditional celebrations

Provide reliable, on-time delivery so customers feel calm and stress-free
